Sage — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Sage is developed and operated by Frank Filius, based in the Netherlands.

What data we collect

Sage stores your data locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This includes your saved philosophers, conversation sessions, memory layers, and preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

When you use Sage, your questions and conversation text are sent to Anthropic (anthropic.com) to generate responses. Anthropic processes this data according to their own privacy policy. We do not store your conversation content on our servers.

Philosopher profiles are stored in our database (Supabase) as public knowledge content. This data is not personal.

If you subscribe to Sage Plus, payment is processed by RevenueCat and the relevant app store (Apple App Store or Google Play). We do not handle or store payment information directly.

What data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information. We do not track your behavior across apps or websites. We do not sell data to third parties.

Data retention

Your local data persists until you clear it manually via Settings or uninstall the app. Clearing memories or sessions in Settings is permanent and cannot be undone.

Your rights under GDPR

As a Netherlands-based service, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation. Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, most GDPR rights apply to data held locally on your device, which you control directly. For any questions or requests, contact us at info@sage-council-app.com.
